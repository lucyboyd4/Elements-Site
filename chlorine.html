Background

Backgroundx

Hydrochloric acid (HCl) was known to the alchemists. By heating hydrochloric acid with the mineral pyrolusite which is naturally occuring manganese dioxide, MnO2, A dense, greenish-yellow gas was evolved which was recorded as having a choking smell and also dissolved in water to give an acid solution. It bleached litmus paper, and decolourised leaves and flowers.

Uses and Discovery

Uses and Discoveryx

Chlorine kills bacteria – it is a disinfectant. It is used to treat drinking water and swimming pool water. It is also used to make hundreds of consumer products such as paper, paints, textiles and insecticides.

In 1774 Chlorine was discovered by Carl Wilhelm Scheele .
