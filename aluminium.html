BACKGROUND +

History of the element

Backgroundx

Aluminium is the world’s most abundant metal and is the third most common element making up 8% of the earth’s crust. The versatility of aluminium makes it the most widely used metal after steel. It is one of the lightest engineering metals.

USES AND DISCOVERY +

Who and what?

Uses and Discoveryx

It is used in a huge variety of products such as cans, kitchen utensils, window frames, foils and aeroplane parts. It is the second most malleable metal and the sixth most ductile.

In 1825 Aluminium was discovered by Hans Oersted.
