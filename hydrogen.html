Background

Backgroundx

Hydrogen is an element that exists naturally as a molecule. Each hydrogen molecule is made up of two hydrogen atoms. It is one of the elements that exists in nature and has one atom that can't be broken down into other substances.

Uses and Discovery

Uses and Discoveryx

Hydrogen is used as fuel and in hydrogen cars it reacts with oxygen in a fuel cell, making electricity to run the car. The only waste product of this process is water vapour.

In 1766 Hydrogen was discovered by Robert Boyle.
